#### in NON-RELATIVISTIC frame (PHYS031)
$$
    \begin{align*}
    E&=W=\int{\vec{F}\cdot{d}\vec{x}}=\int{\left(m\vec{a}\right)\cdot{d}\vec{x}}\\
    &=\int{m\left(\frac{d\vec{v}}{dt}\right)\cdot\vec{x}}=\int{m\left(\frac{d\vec{x}}{dt}\right)d\vec{v}}\\

    \end{align*}
$$
